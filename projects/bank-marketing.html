Bank Marketing Prediction Model

Machine learning model predicting whether a customer will subscribe to a bank term deposit.

Problem

Marketing campaigns generate large customer datasets. The challenge is identifying customers most likely to subscribe.

Solution

Built classification models using scikit-learn to predict deposit subscription based on customer demographics, campaign history, and financial indicators.

Technologies

  • Python
  • Pandas
  • Scikit-Learn
  • Machine Learning
